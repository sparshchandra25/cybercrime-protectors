Types of Cybercrimes

Select the type of cybercrime you want to report. All reports are handled with utmost confidentiality.

🔒

Online Blackmail

Being threatened or coerced online with sensitive information or content.

💰

Money-related Scams

Fraudulent schemes designed to steal money through online transactions or investments.

🚫

Inappropriate Images

Unauthorized sharing or posting of private or inappropriate images online.

📵

Scam Calls

Fraudulent phone calls attempting to extract personal information or money.

👁️

Cyberstalking

Persistent unwanted online contact, harassment, or monitoring of your activities.

🔐

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of your personal information to commit fraud or other crimes.

🎣

Phishing

Deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information through fake websites or emails.
